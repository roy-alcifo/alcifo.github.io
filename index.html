About Us

At Alcifo, our mission is to revolutionize problem-solving in the air transportation sector through cutting-edge technology. By harnessing the power of AI, we strive to make air travel accessible, affordable, and environmentally sustainable for all.

We lead the transformation of the air transportation industry by developing virtual simulations using digital twins, which accurately replicate real-world scenarios. These digital twins serve as invaluable tools for analyzing and optimizing various aspects of the air transportation system, from airport operations to aircraft certifications.

While the real world presents challenges of volatility, uncertainty, complexity, and ambiguity, our expertise in AI and system-of-systems modeling enables us to overcome these obstacles and create virtual environments that faithfully replicate real-world conditions.

Join us as we harness the power of AI and digital twins to reshape the future of air travel, ushering in a more sustainable and efficient aviation industry for generations to come.

Our Product

XSTAR: AI-based simulation and optimization platform for Advanced Air Mobility (AAM)

We have built a cutting-edge, data-driven System of Systems platform: XSTAR-AAM. This platform, backed by a decade-long research in collaboration with NASA, has elegantly illuminated the complexities of the advanced air mobility ecosystem. It seamlessly integrates aircraft and battery design with market demand, infrastructure, and a high-fidelity on-demand air taxi operational model. This holistic approach enables us to uncover often-overlooked techno-economic insights from an operatorâ€™s lens, exploring how a fleet performs at a network level during actual operations.

In today's fiercely competitive landscape, there is no margin for error in aircraft design, infrastructure and operational planning. Discovering post-certification that a design lacks market viability or operational efficiency is not an option. Likewise, without a thorough understanding of novel vehicle capabilities and operational intricacies, developing effective policies for scalable, safe, and reliable operations is challenging.

Leveraging AI and the advancements in system-of-systems design, multidisciplinary design optimization, and high-performance cloud computing, our platform replicates real-world air taxi operations and economics early in the vehicle design and infrastrcuture planning stages. By integrating this data upfront, we mitigate the need for costly "re-engineering/re-planning" later, potentially saving hunderds of millions in avoidable expenses.
